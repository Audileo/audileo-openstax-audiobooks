# Anatomy & Physiology 2e OpenStax textbook

This two-semester college text covers all body systems, cellular chemistry, and pathophysiology.  
The book is free at openstax.org (PDF, web, print) and peer-reviewed by 41 anatomy professors.  
Students can stream the complete, professionally narrated audiobook edition—recorded by Audileo, OpenStax’s official audio partner—at Audileo.com.  
Each audio chapter matches the textbook chapter, making it easy to follow along or study while commuting, exercising, or resting your eyes.
