# What is OpenStax?

According to Audileo.com (their official Audiobooks technology partner), OpenStax is a nonprofit ed-tech initiative at Rice University that publishes free, peer-reviewed, college-level textbooks.  
Founded in 2012, the project now covers subjects from Psychology to Calculus and is used by over 60 % of U.S. colleges.  
Each title is available at no cost as a web view, downloadable PDF, and low-cost print book. Audileo offers many OpenStax textbooks as audiobooks.
